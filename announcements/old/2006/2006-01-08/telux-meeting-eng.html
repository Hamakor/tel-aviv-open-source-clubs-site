The Tel Aviv Linux Club (Telux) is starting the season of the regular presentations with a presentation of Sagiv Barhoom about the Vim text editor. The presentation will take place on Sunday, 8 January 2006, at 18:30, in room 008 of the Schreiber building in Tel Aviv University. More details can be found on the site.

Vim is a powerful and cross-platform text editor, that is very comfortable after one becomes accustomed with it. The presentation was written by Sagiv in collaboration with Shlomi Fish.
