The Tel Aviv Linux Club (Telux) will gather again to hear the presentation of Herouth Maoz about GIMP 2.2. GIMP is a powerful image manipulation program for UNIXes, Windows and Mac OS X.

The presentation will take place on Sunday, 12 March 2006, at 18:30, in room 008 of the Schreiber building in Tel Aviv University. More details can be found on the site.

Attendance is free of charge, and everyone are welcome.
